Use Case Clarity

A template page should say who the workspace is for, what mess it replaces, and what the buyer can do after duplicating it.

Preview Strength

Screenshots should not be decorative. They should show the workflow, dashboard sections, inputs, outputs, and first action.

Buyer Fit

The page should explain skill level, setup time, required apps, included instructions, and who should not buy it.

Quick self-check for Notion template pages

Use these checks before posting the template to a directory, marketplace, product page, profile bio, or launch thread.

  • 1The headline says the job the template helps with, not only the template name.
  • 2The first screen makes the before-state and after-state obvious.
  • 3The page shows enough of the template for a buyer to trust the structure.
  • 4The CTA explains whether the buyer gets instant access, duplicate instructions, updates, or support.
  • 5The page includes one concrete workflow example, not only a list of features.
