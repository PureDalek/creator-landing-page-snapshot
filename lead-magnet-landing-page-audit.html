Trust Before Email

Next-Step Path

A lead magnet page should tell the visitor what happens after opt-in and how the free resource connects to the paid offer.

Quick self-check for lead magnet pages

Use these checks before sending traffic from social posts, newsletter swaps, directory listings, podcast notes, or a profile bio.

  • 1The headline names the useful outcome, not only the file type.
  • 2The page gives enough preview detail to justify the email ask.
  • 3The form CTA says what the visitor receives and how soon.
  • 4The page includes a simple fit note so the right visitors opt in and the wrong ones self-select out.
  • 5The post-opt-in path is clear: read, reply, buy, book, use a tool, or get the deeper resource.
