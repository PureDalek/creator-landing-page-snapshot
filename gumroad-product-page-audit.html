Quick self-check before sending traffic

Use these checks before sharing your Gumroad link in a newsletter, post, bio, marketplace listing, paid ad, or launch thread.

  • 1The product title says the use case, not only a clever name.
  • 2The first paragraph names the buyer, the problem, and the outcome in plain language.
  • 3The page shows what buyers actually receive: file type, length, sections, examples, or screenshots.
  • 4The buy button is supported by timing, refund scope, requirements, or delivery expectations.
  • 5The page gives one next step after purchase so the buyer knows how to use the product.
