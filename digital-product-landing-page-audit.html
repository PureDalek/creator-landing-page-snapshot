Quick self-check for digital product pages

Use these checks before sending product traffic from a newsletter, profile link, marketplace listing, community post, or launch page.

  • 1The headline names the buyer and the practical job the product helps them finish.
  • 2The page shows what is inside before asking for the buy click.
  • 3The CTA says what happens next: instant download, order form, page URL submission, or manual delivery.
  • 4The proof is specific: screenshots, example pages, before-and-after copy, creator type, or a short workflow.
  • 5The page makes scope clear so buyers do not expect coaching, support, software, or guaranteed revenue.
